Data 1

Used library:
plotly

Data source:
Kaggle - Palmer Archipelago (Antarctica) penguin data

The data contains information about Sexual Dimorphism and Environmental Variability within a Community of Antarctic Penguins. The plot I've decided to use is a boxplot, which shows differences in body mass between penguin species and sex.

Data 2

Used library:
plotly

Data source:
Kaggle - IMDB Dataset of top 1000 movies and tv shows.

The data contains the information about top movies and tv shows scored on IMDB. The plot I've decided to use is the pie plot, which shows the genres of the top movies. The important thing to notice is that the movie/show could have more than 1 genre, so all genres will be counted to the figure.
